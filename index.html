What is the difference between UI and UX design?

UI design focuses on the visual elements and layout of a product, while UX design focuses on the overall experience and usability.

Why is UX design important?

UX design is crucial because it ensures that a product is easy to use, meets user needs, and provides a positive experience, leading to higher user satisfaction and retention.

What are key principles of good UI design?

Key principles include consistency, clarity, simplicity, feedback, and accessibility, ensuring that interfaces are intuitive and user-friendly.
